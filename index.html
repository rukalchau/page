Courses

I offer comprehensive programming and machine learning courses that are specifically designed for teenagers who have a keen interest in the exciting fields of computer science and artificial intelligence (AI). In today's technology-driven world, understanding the fundamentals of coding and AI is not only a valuable skill but also a gateway to countless opportunities.

My courses are structured to provide a solid foundation in programming, with a strong emphasis on Python. Python is an excellent starting point, known for its simplicity and versatility, making it a perfect choice for beginners. In addition to Python, I introduce students to micro:bit, a user-friendly and hands-on platform that allows them to explore physical computing. Micro:bit is a great way for students to apply their programming knowledge to create interactive and tangible projects, which can be a highly motivating and engaging aspect of learning.

Moreover, I incorporate Scratch, a visual programming language, into my courses. Scratch is an ideal tool for teenagers as it offers a creative and playful approach to coding, allowing students to build their programming skills through interactive and entertaining projects. It's a great way to ignite a passion for coding and computational thinking.

With my background in mathematics, I am well-equipped to provide students with the intuitive understanding of the mathematical principles that underpin computer science and AI. This is crucial because mathematics is the language of these fields, and a solid grasp of mathematical concepts can significantly enhance a student's ability to comprehend and excel in programming and AI.

My courses are not just about teaching code but also fostering problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and creativity. As students progress, they will have the opportunity to delve into the fascinating world of machine learning, a subfield of AI that powers many of today's technological advancements. By demystifying machine learning concepts and providing hands-on experience, I aim to prepare students for the AI-driven future and empower them to be creators and innovators in this field.

If you or your teenager is interested in exploring the world of computer science and AI, I encourage you to get in touch with me to learn more about the courses I offer. Together, we can embark on a journey of discovery, learning, and growth in these exciting and ever-evolving fields.
